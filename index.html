      What is Dinosaur's Fruits!

      Play Now!

      Dinosaur's Fruits is a fun and colouful arcade game where you control a friendly dinosaur moving left and right to catch falling apples and strawberries. The game is set across two exciting levels- a peaceful forest and a challenging desert Savanna. Wtch out for ghost obstacles and try to reach the target score to win each level! Perfect for players of all ages, especially children who love bright cartoon games.
