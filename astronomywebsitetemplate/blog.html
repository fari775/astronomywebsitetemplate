    Level 1- Forest Adventure

    Begin your journey in a beautiful forest with a bright blue sky. This is the perfect starting level — apples fall slowly, giving you plenty of time to learn the controls and get comfortable with the game. The forest background features rolling green hills and a peaceful atmosphere.Catch 10 apples before they hit the ground to complete Level 1 and unlock the Desert Challenge. Watch out — a ghost may appear even here!    

  • 22-05-2026 

    Level 2 Desert Challenge

    Welcome to the Desert Savana- the ultimate tast of your dinosaur skills! Fruits now fall much faster and from more random positions across the screen. Both appels AND strawberries appear, so you need tp move quickly and think fast. Collect 20 fruits to comlete Level 2 and win the game. The ghost is faster and more unpredicatable here - stay sharp and keep moving.

  • 22-05-2025

    Explanation

    The player moves and changes scenes by controlling the Left arrow key (move dinosaur left) and the Right arrow key (move dinosaur right). There are also Manu screen with buttons: Play, Instructions, Exit. After pressing Play, choose a level. At the end of a level, buttons allow the next level, play again or menu. We have as well Scene changes like Menu to Instructions to Menu. Also, Menu to Level 1 to Level 2, and then any level to Game over to Menu or play again.
