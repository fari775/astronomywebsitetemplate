How to Play

Step-by-Step Guide Step1: Start the Game Open the game and you will see the main menu with a starry background. Click Play Game to begin your adventure!

Step 2: Choose a levelSelect Level 1 if you are a beginner — it is slower and easier. When you are ready, try Level 2 for a bigger challenge!

Step 3: Move Your Dinosaur Use the Left ← arrow key to move Dino left and the Right → arrow key to move Dino right along the bottom of the screen.

Step 4: Ctach the Fruits Position your dinosaur underneath the falling apples and strawberries to catch them. Each fruit caught adds +1 to your score!

Step 5: Avoid the Ghost Ghost obstacles appear on screen. Do not touch them! If your dinosaur hits a ghost, the game ends immediately — Game Over!

Step 6: Reach The Target Catch 10 fruits to complete Level 1 and 20 fruits to complete Level 2 and win the whole game. You got this! 

  • Apple

    The main collectible in both levels. Catch each apple before it hits the ground! +1Score

  • Strawberry

    Appears in Level 2 alongside apples. Faster and in more random positions! +1 Score

  • Ghost Obstacle

    Roams the screen as a dangerous obstacle. Touch it and the game is over! Gam Over

  • Missed Fruit

    Missing a fruit that hits the ground reduces your energy. Don't miss too many! -1 Energy
